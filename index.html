O nama

Agency for PR and Media Consulting Headline was founded eleven years ago, in January 2008. Agency is co-owner and partner of Digital agency Dkit, founded by the great Israeli agency Isocia. D-kit is agency specialized in digital communication, promotion and advertising in digital media, founded in 2012.

Headline & Dkit consists of experienced team of experts with professional skills in the area of media and public relations, consulting, event management, public affairs, brand advisory, marketing, digital strategies and solutions.

By combining knowledge about media, and experience in working with domestic international clients, while knowing the rules of online and offline communication and change of people's habits in the field of modern technology - we precisely determine who we are addresing to, how to create and customize content, and what kind of messages to send.
